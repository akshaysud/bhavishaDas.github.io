Identity, Values and Strengths

Core Personal Values

One of my core values is work ethic and being honest to the work I undertake. I really don't appriciate when people don't follow up with their commitments.
When the lockdown started, we in the team knew that we will be made redundant soon. Before that though, we had a heap of bookings which were supposed to be cancelled. Being in sales, cancelled bookings meant losing on a heap of money along with the redundancy looming.
I had a to make a decision. It was either to sit at home and enjoy the wage subsidy knowing that I was close to loosing my job OR front up the harsh reality and get the work done
I chose the later and it felt right. Didn't make a change in my paycheck but I felt I had done the right thing.
I feel that this comes from growing up in a household where I have seen everyone hustle. I feel greatful to have grown up in an atomosphere where hardwork is recognised.
